# Collaboration: Building Together
At Gazelle, we believe in the power of collaboration.
We understand that the strength of a framework lies not only in its codebase but also in the community that surrounds it.
That's why we invite developers from all backgrounds and skill levels to join us on our journey to create the best possible web development experience with Dart.

## Your Feedback Matters
Your feedback is invaluable to us.
Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting out, your insights into
using Gazelle can help us identify areas for improvement and refine our features to better suit your needs.
We welcome clear and honest feedback, so don't hesitate to reach out to us through our community channels or GitHub repository.

## Contributing Code
If you're passionate about improving Gazelle and have coding skills to contribute, we'd love to have you on board.
Whether it's fixing bugs, implementing new features, or optimizing performance, every contribution makes a difference.
Check out our GitHub repository for open issues and contribute your code to push Gazelle forward.

## Creating Plugins
Plugins are the heart of Gazelle's extensibility, and we believe in the creativity and innovation of our community.
If you have an idea for a plugin that could enhance Gazelle's functionality or simplify common tasks, we encourage you to bring it to life.
Share your plugins with the community and contribute to making Gazelle even more powerful and versatile.

## Join the Community
Beyond code contributions, our community is a place for learning, sharing ideas, and supporting one another.
Join our forums, participate in discussions, and connect with fellow developers who share your passion for Dart and web development.
Together, we can revolutionize backend development with Gazelle and shape the future of Dart programming.

At Gazelle, we believe that by collaborating and working together, we can achieve great things.
Join us in building a vibrant and inclusive community around Gazelle, where developers of all backgrounds can come together to learn, create, and innovate.
Together, we can make Gazelle the go-to framework for building modern web applications with Dart.
