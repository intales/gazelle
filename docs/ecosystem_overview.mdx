# Ecosystem Overview

Plugins are the heart and soul of the Gazelle ecosystem and its vibrant community.
They empower developers to collaborate seamlessly and scale their projects effortlessly.
By creating plugins, developers can harness the collective expertise of the community to build powerful tools that simplify the life of every developer.
With Gazelle plugins, the possibilities are endless, and the journey of web development becomes even more exciting and accessible for everyone involved.

Here you will learn how to write a plugin, but first, let's see how they're
managed by Gazelle!
